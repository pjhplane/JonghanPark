Squad Leader, Infantry, Republic of Korea

I served army in General Outpost (GOP) where is a boundary of North and South Korea as a guard to prevent the enemy’s sudden attack for 21 months. I was a squad leader who lead a squad to carry out the mission. After carrying out the mission in GOP, my platoon received ‘Best Military Operation in General Outpost’ by 3rd Battalion Commander. Through these experiences, I learned how to lead a group and how to communicate and act with group members.
